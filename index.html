About Me

Hello. I am a recent graduate from University of California, Irvine with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. I am originally from Milpitas, CA but am currently looking for a job around the Irvine area! While in college, I have taken classes with both Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. The goal of my career is to be a full-stack software engineer. I am also interested in data engineering and data analytics. I came into college not knowing what exactly I wanted to do, but that I wanted to create things and work with computers, hence why I chose Computer Engineering since it encompasses both the hardware and software side. My interest in the Computer Science aspect of Computer Engineering only grew as I became more exposed to the possibilities through classes, YouTube, and friends. I've even had the pleasure of working on some cool projects myself such as a photo/video filter application and a robotic hand that was controlled by a haptic feedback glove. This website is a fun little project to help myself learn pure HTML/CSS while also serving as a portfolio for myself. Throughout college, I learned MySQL, Java, C, C++, and SolidWorks, but I am now self - learning Python, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript.
